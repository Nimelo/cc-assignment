\begin{abstract}
	Cloud computing is very popular nowadays and used by huge companies such as \gls{adobe},  \gls{docker} or \gls{netflix}. Solutions provided by cloud services are very convenient for their client. One of the biggest and most popular cloud companies is \gls{AWS}.
	
	This report summarizes work and approach used in terms of developing of a collaborative platform for archival and sharing of student coursework. Used service will be a \gls{git} based solution with a very advance web application -- \gls{gitlab}. Platform is configured and designed for \gls{AWS}.
\end{abstract}
